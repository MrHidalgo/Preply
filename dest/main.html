What kind of tutors does Preply look for?

No specific certification or teaching experience is required! We welcome English tutors who:

  • Enjoy sharing knowledge and making a difference in students’ lives
  • Have outstanding communication skills
  • Are willing to provide a personalized learning experience to international students

How do you become a tutor?

It’s easy to start and easy to earn! Sign up and create a tutor profile.

We’ll check it within 5 business days. Once your profile is approved, students from around the world will see it on Preply and will be able to book lessons with you. Also, you’ll have access to useful tools, like training webinars and teaching materials for your lessons.

Learn more about how to create a profile here

Why should I teach on Preply?

  • An opportunity to earn by sharing what you know
  • A steady stream of new students looking for English tutors
  • Convenient tools for managing your lessons and connecting with students
  • Safe payment methods (Paypal, Payoneer or Skrill)
  • Learning plans and tests to save you time on lesson preparation
  • Support from our friendly team through training webinars, live chat and email

What are the technology requirements for Preply tutors?

You will need a stable internet connection, a webcam, and a microphone for conducting lessons in the virtual classroom.

Is it free to create a tutor profile on Preply?

Yes. It is free to create a tutor profile, get exposure to students, and use Preply’s tools and materials.

We only charge a commission when a tutor starts to get bookings from students.

How much can I earn on Preply?

Most popular tutors on Preply earn up to $550 a week. Your earnings depend on the hourly rate you set, the number of lessons you teach and how many students continue learning with you after the trial lesson. A tip for newly registered tutors: start with a lower hourly rate to get first students faster. You can change your rate anytime.

Preply takes a commission fee from your lessons, and uses it to bring in more students from around the globe and develop an easy-to-use video tool and learning materials for your lessons. We provide free training webinars and multilingual customer support to guide you along every step of your tutoring journey.

The commission for every trial lesson with a new student is 100%. For all subsequent lessons, the commission starts at 33% and decreases to 18% based on how many hours you’ve taught on the platform.
